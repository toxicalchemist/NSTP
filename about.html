Our History

Barangay Talipapa was founded and named by Lieutenant General Manuel Pavia y Marquez de Novaliches last 1854, who was then assigned by the Spanish Government as Philippine Governor General. His objective then was to give positive name for identification of the areas he visited. The name Barrio Talipapa came up to him when he visited a store in this place called Talipapa, after which Barrio Talipapa (new Barangay Talipapa) was created. Sitios under this Barrio are San Agustin, Sangandaan, Sauyo, Libis and Kabaruhan. Significant historical event took place in Barrio Talipapa, where in San Agustin the first encounter between the Spanish Government and the son of Kapitinan Andres Bonifacio with Emilio Jacinto, and the Mother of the Katipunan, Melchora Aquino or Tandang Sora was born in Barrio Talipapa.
